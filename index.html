About

My name is Joshua Mines and I’m a software Engineer who loves using data and algorithms to better understand our world. Right now I’m working on an app that will help vessel navigators plan safe and efficient voyages. Besides a love for software I also love reading and am an amateur strongman (heavy emphasis on amateur).

Before coding I was an officer in the US Merchant Marine. While the maritime industry does utilize software for stability calculations and navigation there are a plethora of other tasks that are still done manually. As a 3rd Mate my safety inspections were done on a piece of paper which was then scanned and saved in the computer, as a 2nd Mate the voyage abstracts I created were manually entered into word documents and posted in the wheelhouse. It was problems like these that inspired me to look into software development as a career. If I could eliminate redundancy, error and unnecessary work it would not only free up time for the crew but would result in a safer voyage. To help me hone my coding skills I joined DevMountain’s. Python Software Engineering Program.

Since graduating I have spent my time creating projects and adding to my coding knowledge. With every new language or feature I learn I see another way to tackle the problems I saw offshore. I look forward to career and tech and to being part of a team where I can contribute and learn.
